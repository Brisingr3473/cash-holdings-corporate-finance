# Cash Holdings and Firm Performance After the Great Recession

## Overview
This project studies whether pre-existing cash balances helped surviving U.S. technology firms perform during and after the Great Recession.

## Research Question
 How did cash holdings affect performance among surviving U.S. technological firms during and after the Great Recession?

## Methods
- Corporate finance ratio analysis
- Cross-sectional regressions
- Performance comparisons
- Financial statement analysis

## Key Findings
Our results did not find any evidence that cash holding positions prior to the recession had an impact on firm performance during and after the recession.

## Tools Used
Excel, Stata, Capital IQ Pro, Financial Statement Data
